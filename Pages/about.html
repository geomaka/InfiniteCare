Who We Are

InfiniteCare Disability Centre and Innovation Hub is a non-profit educational research and community service centre for persons with disabilities (PWDs).

The InfiniteCare Centre is a well-equipped one-stop centre offering a variety of services under highly qualified and experienced professionals. We host modern facilities for sensory integration, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation managed by experienced specialists.

Our Vision

A world without barriers to service provision and participation.

Our Mission

To build an innovation ecosystem that is inclusive by creating solutions that are accessible to all and better than before, for persons with disability and in all sectors.
