EARC & Physiotherapy Services

Specialized evaluations, early interventions, and rehabilitation for individuals with disabilities — across all ages.

Modern Private Education Assessment and Resource Centre (EARC)

InfiniteCare offers expert evaluations and tailored interventions for individuals with disabilities through a multidisciplinary approach. Services include:

  • Functional Assessment – In-depth evaluations to identify strengths, challenges, and support strategies.
  • Early Intervention & Rehabilitation – Timely support for children with developmental delays.
  • Speech Therapy – Enhance communication and language skills.
  • Physiotherapy – Improve mobility, posture, and coordination.
  • Sensory Integration & CBT – Coping strategies for emotional and sensory regulation.
  • School & Home Education Support – Personalized learning plans for special needs education.

Physiotherapy Services

We offer tailored treatments for restoring strength, reducing pain, and improving mobility. Each plan is created based on patient evaluation and goals.

Conditions We Treat

  • Lumbar Spondylosis & Post-Traumatic Injury
  • Osteoarthritis / Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Bell’s / Facial Palsy, Stroke, Hemiparesis
  • Post-Surgical Recovery & Edema Management
  • Plantar Fasciitis & Neuromuscular Rehab

Pediatric Physiotherapy

We specialize in developmental support for children with:

  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Spina Bifida
  • Rickets & Muscle Weakness
  • Meningitis & Hydrocephalus
  • Down Syndrome & Other Neurological Conditions

Our goal is to enhance mobility, coordination, and independence through play-based and functional therapies tailored to each child's needs.
