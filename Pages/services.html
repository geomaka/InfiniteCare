Our Services

At Infinitecare Disability Centre and Innovation Hub, our comprehensive services are tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals with disabilities across the lifespan, promoting independence, inclusion, and quality of life.

Functional Assessment

Our team utilizes evidence-based tools and methodologies to conduct thorough functional assessments, forming the foundation for personalized intervention plans.

Education Support

Tailored in-school and at-home educational support ensuring accommodations, resources, and assistance for every child to thrive academically and socially.

Early Intervention

We focus on timely identification and evidence-based early support to maximize the developmental potential of children with disabilities.

Physiotherapy

Designed to improve mobility, strength, and function, our physiotherapy services are customized to individual goals and physical abilities.

Occupational Therapy

We promote independence in daily life through skill-building, assistive devices, and environment adaptations tailored to each person.

Speech Therapy

Enhancing communication skills through individualized speech, language, and social communication interventions.

Play Therapy

Guided play sessions help children express emotions, develop relationships, and build coping strategies in a safe environment.

Guidance and Counselling

Confidential support for individuals and families dealing with emotional and social challenges, building resilience and setting life goals.

Youth Engagement

Empowering youth with disabilities through vocational training, leadership development, recreation, and peer support programs.

Family Empowerment

We provide education, support groups, and resources to help families navigate challenges and advocate effectively for their loved ones.
